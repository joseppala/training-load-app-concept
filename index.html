Training load app concept

Today's busy lifestyle combined with ambitious exercise often leads to overtraining. With this app you can easily see when you are overtraining and compromising recovery.

Interactions with the app are done through conversations. Each day the app will ask you a few questions about your sleep and stress and based on the answers it will calculate how much they are contributing to your total load. The user can also launch conversations with the app to report workouts and to get advice.
